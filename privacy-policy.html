Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

SERP Tracker Pro ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that checks domain rankings in Google search results. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

The Extension collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Domains you enter in the search field (stored in browser localStorage for quick access history)
  • Your Pro license key, if you choose to activate one (stored in browser localStorage)

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

2. How the Extension Works

When you perform a rank check, the Extension makes requests to Google Search using your existing browser session. These requests are made directly from your browser — no data passes through our servers. The Extension reads the search results page to extract ranking information and displays it to you.

3. License Validation

If you activate a Pro license, the Extension communicates with LemonSqueezy solely to validate your license key. No personal data beyond the license key itself is transmitted. Please refer to LemonSqueezy's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle this data.

4. Permissions

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab — to read the current Google search results page
  • scripting — to extract ranking data from the page

These permissions are used solely to provide the rank-checking functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Data Retention

All data stored by the Extension (domain history, license key) remains on your device and can be cleared at any time by removing the Extension or clearing your browser's local storage.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our store page.
