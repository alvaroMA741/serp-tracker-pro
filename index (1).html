FAQ

Common questions

How does it work without an API?

The extension uses your existing Google browser session to fetch search results pages directly. No API keys, no third-party services involved in the rank checking process.

Does it work on any Google search?

Yes. Open any Google search results page on desktop, click the extension, enter a domain and hit Check Rank. It works with any keyword and any Google domain.

How does position tracking work?

Every time you check a domain for a keyword, the extension saves the result locally. The next time you check the same domain and keyword, it compares the positions and shows you if you've moved up (green ↑) or down (red ↓).

What does "2 activations" mean for Pro?

Your Pro license can be active on 2 devices simultaneously — for example your work computer and your laptop. You can deactivate and reactivate at any time.

Is the Pro license a subscription?

No. It's a one-time payment of €6.90. You pay once and own it forever, including all future updates.

What data does the extension collect?

Nothing is sent to our servers. Domain history, position history and license key are stored locally in your browser. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
